The Best Movies According to Usama Sandhu

My top 3 movies of all time

The Hobbit

The Hobbit is a 2012 epic fantasy adventure film directed by Peter Jackson. It is the first installment in a three-part film adaptation based on J. R. R. Tolkien's 1937 novel The Hobbit, and serves as a prequel to Jackson's The Lord of the Rings film trilogy. The film stars Martin Freeman as Bilbo Baggins, alongside an ensemble cast including Ian McKellen, Richard Armitage, Benedict Cumberbatch, Evangeline Lilly, Lee Pace, Orlando Bloom, and Hugo Weaving.

Borne Series

The Bourne series is a collection of action-thriller films based on the character Jason Bourne, a former CIA assassin suffering from amnesia. The series includes five films: "The Bourne Identity" (2002), "The Bourne Supremacy" (2004), "The Bourne Ultimatum" (2007), "The Bourne Legacy" (2012), and "Jason Bourne" (2016). Starring Matt Damon as Jason Bourne in the first three and the fifth film, and Jeremy Renner in the fourth, the series is known for its intense action sequences, espionage themes, and complex storytelling.

Dune

Dune is a 2021 epic science fiction film directed by Denis Villeneuve, based on the 1965 novel of the same name by Frank Herbert. The film features an ensemble cast including Timothée Chalamet, Rebecca Ferguson, Oscar Isaac, Josh Brolin, Stellan Skarsgård, Zendaya, and Jason Momoa. Set in a distant future where noble houses vie for control of the desert planet Arrakis, the only source of the universe's most valuable substance, "spice," Dune explores themes of politics, religion, and ecology.
